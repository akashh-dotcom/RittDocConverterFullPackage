Thank you for submitting your PDA collection to convert your R2 Digital Library trial to an active PDA account. You are now able to implement this powerful collection development tool into your library's digital acquisition strategy.

You can continue to add titles to your PDA collection and monitor your collection's activity via the Admin area of the R2 Digital Library. To ensure that your library gets the most out of the R2 Digital Library, please be sure to:

  • Choose your authentication methods - IP validation provides seamless integration on site, and you can also provide remote access for your users by enabling individual accounts
  • Confirm that your IP ranges are complete and loaded correctly
  • Review R2 Digital Library integration tools, including persistent URLs and MARC downloads
  • Ensure that your Athens credentials, Federated Search and Referrer URLs are configured correctly (if applicable)
  • Sign up for email notifications, including PDA Alerts, New eBook Notifications, New Edition Notifications and more
  • Upload your branding image and welcome message
  • Utilize R2 Digital Library Outreach Materials to inform and train your staff and patrons
  • Contact your commercial A-Z list provider with your collection details (if applicable)

Please remember that PDA titles accessed more than two times will automatically be moved into an R2 Digital Library shopping cart for your review and revert back to table of contents-only access. If you do not purchase PDA titles within 30 days of a trigger event, PDA titles are automatically removed from your shopping cart.

To view your PDA collection, log in to the R2 Digital Library and select "My PDA Collection" within the Admin tab. To view a complete history of your PDA collection, select "My PDA History."

If you have any questions related to your R2 Digital Library PDA account, please email customerservice@r2library.com and we'll be happy to help.
